About ZeeVision

Building the future of identity verification

We're on a mission to make identity verification accessible, secure, and seamless for businesses across Africa. Our team combines deep expertise in AI, security, and compliance to deliver solutions that build trust in Africa's digital economy.

Our Mission

Enabling trust in Africa's digital economy

In Africa's rapidly growing digital economy, verifying identity has become both more critical and more challenging. We believe that secure identity verification shouldn't come at the cost of user experience.

Founded in Lagos, our mission is to provide African businesses with the tools they need to verify identities quickly, accurately, and compliantly—while delivering a seamless experience that users appreciate.
