Frequently asked questions

Have questions about our pricing? Find answers to common questions below.

What happens if I exceed my monthly verification limit?

If you exceed your monthly limit, additional verifications are billed at our overage rate. We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit so there are no surprises. For consistent high volume, we recommend upgrading to a higher tier plan for better value. All pricing is in Nigerian Naira (₦).

Can I change my plan at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll have immediate access to new features. When downgrading, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Annual plan changes may be subject to prorated adjustments.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with 50 verifications included. No credit card required to start. You'll have access to all features including NIN and BVN verification during the trial period so you can fully evaluate our platform before committing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve), bank transfers, and Paystack/Flutterwave payments. Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice with NET 30 terms.

Do you offer custom pricing for high volume?

Absolutely. For businesses processing more than 10,000 verifications per month, we offer volume discounts. Contact our sales team to discuss a custom pricing plan tailored to your specific needs and volume requirements.

What's included in the Enterprise plan?

Enterprise plans include unlimited verifications, dedicated account management, custom SLA with guaranteed uptime, on-premise deployment options, advanced analytics, priority feature development, 24/7 phone support, and full CBN/NDPR compliance reporting.
